  • 5-Marketing : With the shift to online and digital channels, skills in areas like social media marketing, SEO, SEM, and content marketing have been key for businesses to reach their target audience.

  • 7-Internet of Things (IoT) : Skills in IoT, including hardware, software development, and data analysis for connected devices, have been important as IoT continues to expand.
  • 8-Remote Work and Collaboration Tools : Proficiency in tools like Zoom, Slack, Microsoft Teams, and project management software has been crucial for remote work and collaboration.
  • 9-UX/UI Design : Design skills focused on user experience (UX) and user interface (UI) have been important for creating intuitive and engaging digital experiences.
  • 10-E-commerce and Online Retail : Skills related to e-commerce platforms, online retail strategies, and digital storefront management have been valuable as e-commerce continues to grow.
